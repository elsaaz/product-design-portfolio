UX Case Study

Silent Drop-Off

Reducing Commuter Anxiety Through UX

Duration 8 weeks
Team 3 people
Role UX/UI Designer
Tools Figma, FigJam

Overview

Silent Drop-Off is a mobile application designed to reduce anxiety for commuters during passenger drop-off situations. The app provides real-time tracking, seamless communication, and a sense of security for both drivers and passengers.

✓ Real-time location tracking
✓ Two-way in-app communication
✓ Safety-focused features
✓ User-centric design approach

The Problem

Commuters experience significant anxiety during drop-off situations due to unclear communication, uncertainty about timing, and safety concerns. Traditional ride-sharing apps focused on the driver's perspective, neglecting the emotional needs of passengers.

Communication Gap

No way to communicate directly with the driver about drop-off location or timing adjustments.

Timing Uncertainty

Passengers didn't know when the driver would arrive, causing anxiety and impatience.

Safety Concerns

Lack of transparency about driver location and trip details made passengers feel unsafe.

Emotional Neglect

Existing solutions ignored the emotional aspects of the drop-off experience.

Research & Insights

User Interviews

15+

Conducted interviews with regular commuters to understand their pain points and needs.

Survey Responses

50+

Gathered quantitative data from surveys about user behaviors and preferences.

Competitive Analysis

2

Analyzed existing solutions to identify gaps and opportunities.

User Personas

3

Created detailed personas representing our target users.

Key Findings

  • Users want real-time updates on driver location and arrival time
  • Anxiety peaks 5-10 minutes before expected arrival
  • Trust is built through transparency and clear communication
  • Mobile-first experience is essential for this demographic
  • Emotional reassurance is as important as functional features

Solution & Design

Based on our research insights, we designed a comprehensive mobile app that addresses both the functional and emotional needs of commuters during drop-off situations.

📍

Real-Time Tracking

Live map showing driver location with live ETA updates and accuracy indicators to reduce uncertainty.

💬

Two-Way Communication

In-app messaging system allowing passengers and drivers to coordinate quickly without phone calls.

🔔

Safety Notifications

Proactive notifications about driver arrival, location changes, and SOS features for added security.

⭐

Ratings & Reviews

Transparent feedback system building trust through community validation and ratings.

4.6/5

Highly rated on app stores reflecting quality and user satisfaction.

Key Learnings

Emotional Design is Critical

Addressing the emotional aspects of user experience is just as important as functional design. Anxiety reduction was a key metric that drove all design decisions.

Real-Time Feedback Builds Trust

Providing real-time information and status updates significantly reduces user anxiety and builds confidence in the service.

Transparency Matters

Users appreciate transparency about driver location, timing, and any changes. It builds trust and reduces uncertainty.

Test with Real Users

Iterative testing with real users revealed insights we couldn't have discovered through research alone.
